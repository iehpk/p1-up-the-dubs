Introduction

Welcome to Up the Dubs, your friendly guide to Dublin City in Ireland.

Before we begin, it is best that we get a baseline – a starting point so that we can always find our way when things go wrong.

The baseline will be The Spire – the giant needle situated in the middle of O’Connell Street which happens to be the main street of Dublin.

Virtually all public transport for Dublin passes by or near The Spire. This means that whichever way you find yourself lost in, you can easily find transport to drop you off close to or at O’Connor Street, then you can make your way towards the Spire to find your bearing and look at that map again.

From O’Connell Street, we can find our way to all the attractions in the city and beyond.
